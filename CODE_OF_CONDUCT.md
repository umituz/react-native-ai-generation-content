# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

- **Be respectful**: Treat others with respect and dignity
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome all contributions and perspectives
- **Be constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and guidance
- **Be collaborative**: Work together to achieve common goals
- **Be empathetic**: Consider others' perspectives and experiences

### Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment**: Sexualized language or imagery, unwelcome sexual attention
- **Derogatory comments**: Insults, derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- **Trolling**: Deliberate provocation or inflammatory comments
- **Discrimination**: Discriminatory jokes, language, or imagery
- **Doxxing**: Publishing others' private information
- **Threats**: Threats of violence or harm

## Our Responsibilities

### Project Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:

1. **Clarifying Standards**: Clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior
2. **Taking Action**: Taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior
3. **Being Responsive**: Responding to any reports of unacceptable behavior promptly and professionally

### Contributors

Contributors are responsible for:

1. **Following Standards**: Adhering to this Code of Conduct
2. **Respecting Others**: Treating all community members with respect
3. **Reporting Issues**: Reporting any violations to maintainers
4. **Accepting Feedback**: Accepting feedback gracefully and working to improve

## Reporting Issues

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

**Email:** [conduct@yourdomain.com](mailto:conduct@yourdomain.com)

**Include in your report:**
- Your contact information
- The incident description
- Any relevant screenshots or logs
- Names of individuals involved (if comfortable)
- Timestamp of the incident

### Confidentiality

All reports will be kept confidential to the extent possible. We will:
- Protect your privacy
- Investigate thoroughly
- Take appropriate action
- Follow up with you

### What Happens Next

1. **Acknowledgment**: We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: We will investigate the incident
3. **Action**: We will take appropriate action based on findings
4. **Follow-up**: We will follow up with you about the outcome

## Enforcement Actions

### Possible Actions

If a violation occurs, we may:

1. **Warning**: Issue a private warning
2. **Removal**: Remove content that violates standards
3. **Suspension**: Temporarily suspend access
4. **Ban**: Permanently ban from the community

### Context Matters

We will consider the context and severity when determining consequences:
- **Impact**: Impact on the community
- **Intent**: Whether the behavior was intentional
- **History**: Previous violations or contributions
- **Remediation**: Willingness to correct behavior
